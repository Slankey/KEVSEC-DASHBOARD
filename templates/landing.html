About

Mission Profile

I'm a cybersecurity professional with hands-on experience in threat intelligence, OSINT research, and infrastructure defense. KEVSEC is my personal security operations platform — a live environment I designed, built, and actively maintain to monitor and respond to real-world threats targeting my infrastructure 24/7.

My work spans network perimeter defense, adversary tradecraft research, malware analysis, and digital forensics. I've built this platform end-to-end: automated threat blocking pipelines, multi-source intelligence aggregation, and a unified operations interface that surfaces what matters in real time.

I approach security as an engineering discipline — every system I build is designed to be measurable, automatable, and continuously improved. The goal is operational clarity: understand what's hitting you, attribute it, and respond faster than the adversary expects.
