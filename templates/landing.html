About

Who We Are

KEVSEC is an independent cyber intelligence and operations platform built for continuous network monitoring, threat aggregation, and active defense research. Built and operated from a hardened homelab running bare-metal Linux infrastructure.

Our stack aggregates 20+ threat intelligence feeds, monitors live CVE disclosures, tracks geomagnetic and solar activity, and maintains real-time situational awareness across network, weather, and geopolitical domains.

All traffic into this infrastructure is logged, analyzed, and cross-referenced against known threat actor IPs. Persistent scanners and credential stuffers are automatically blacklisted via nftables with Cloudflare WAF enforcement at the edge.
